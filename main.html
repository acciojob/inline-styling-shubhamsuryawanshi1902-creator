Flowers photos

Flowers are the specialized reproductive structures of angiosperms, or flowering plants, designed to produce seeds through pollination by wind or animals. They consist of four main parts—sepals, petals, stamens (male), and carpels (female)—and function to attract pollinators to aid in plant reproduction. There are an estimated 250,000 to 400,000 species of flowers
